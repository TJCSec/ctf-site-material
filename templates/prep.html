Preparation

There are a number of ways you can prepare for TJCTF. First and foremost, make sure you have access to a Linux machine. Many of the binaries we will distribute will only run on certain architectures. If you do not already have an installation, you can install a virtual machine using VMware player or Virtual Box. We recommend installing either good â€˜ol Ubuntu, or the more powerful Kali Linux.

Once you have familiarized yourself with these platforms, there are a couple places you can visit for practice. PicoCTF has two years worth of challenges that you can try by signing up on their site. Smash the stack is another great platform for practicing, with a variety of challenges for you to try. And as with any CTF, remember that Google is your best friend.
